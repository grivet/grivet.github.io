Writing checklist

2021-01-29

Useful

Useful writing tells people something true and important that they didn't already know, and tells them as unequivocally as possible. â€“ Paul Graham

Valuable

Writing should be valuable.

Value stems from changing an audience ideas.

Knowing the audience is a prerequisite.

Methodical

  1. Who are you writing to?
  2. Why are you writing to them?
  3. What do you want them to know?
  4. What do they already know?
  5. What style of writing is most effective for this audience and intention?

Decide the first point, then derive others from it.
